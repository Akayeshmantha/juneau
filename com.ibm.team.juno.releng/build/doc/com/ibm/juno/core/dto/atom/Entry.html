Class Entry

  • java.lang.Object
    • com.ibm.juno.core.dto.atom.Common
      • com.ibm.juno.core.dto.atom.CommonEntry
        • com.ibm.juno.core.dto.atom.Entry

  • @Xml(name="entry")
public class Entry
extends CommonEntry
    Represents an atomEntry construct in the RFC4287 specification.

    Schema

    atomEntry = element atom:entry { atomCommonAttributes, (atomAuthor* & atomCategory* & atomContent? & atomContributor* & atomId & atomLink* & atomPublished? & atomRights? & atomSource? & atomSummary? & atomTitle & atomUpdated & extensionElement*) }

    Refer to com.ibm.juno.core.dto.atom for further information about ATOM support.

    Author:
    James Bognar (jbognar@us.ibm.com)
